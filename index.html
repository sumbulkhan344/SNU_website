SNIoE Makerspace

Welcome to SNIoE's Makerspace, an embodiment of our commitment to STEAM education and innovation. Here, we offer a dynamic collaborative environment fostering interdisciplinary research and project creation.

Our Makerspace is equipped with cutting-edge, user-friendly tools and state-of-the art technologies, ensuring accesseibilty and affordability. It serves as hub for world-class reserch test-beds, facilitating the creation of quality publications.

As one-stop destination, our space fuels solutions in sustainability, green energy, intelligent building, robotics, networks, and AI. Additionally, we provide comprehensive training and workshops, ensuring efficient and safe utilizartion tools and machines.

How to use Makerspace







Login / Register in the website

Register yourself with your organization mail and other details

Make an appointment to use a machine

Use our equipment catalogue to select your desired equipment and book a slot.
You cannot an equipment if its not operational or have already been booked by someone else.

Watch the tutorial

Once you book a slot, go through the tutorials suggested in your bookings dashboard for the equipment that you have selected.
You will have to demonstrate your knowledge about the tools to the lab tachnician in order to get your slot approved.
